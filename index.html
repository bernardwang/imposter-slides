So what is

Imposter Syndrome?


There's a stock photo for that...

"A feeling of phoniness in people who believe that they are not intelligent, capable or creative despite evidence of high achievement"

"Phenomenon from an incorrect assessment of ones ability compared to peers"

Who is affected?

  • High achieving people
  • Students, working professionals
  • STEM fields
  • Women, minorities

What are the effects?

  • Increased stress and anxiety
  • Taking actions that can be self limiting
